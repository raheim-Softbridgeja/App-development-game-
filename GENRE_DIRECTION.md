# Genre Direction

This game should borrow proven structure from top match games while keeping its own island-yard identity.

## What the leaders are doing

- Royal Match centers a polished puzzle loop plus renovation and events. Google Play describes coins for unlocking areas, castle decoration, extra boosters, and live events like King's Cup and Team Battle.
  Source: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?hl=en_US&id=com.dreamgames.royalmatch
- Dream Games' official Royal Match page shows why the presentation works: curved, polished forms, environment-themed match items, and booster combinations that feel strategic instead of noisy.
  Source: https://www.dreamgames.com/games/royal-match
- Homescapes proves the room-by-room restoration loop still works when every win feeds house progress, power-ups, and limited-time events.
  Source: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?hl=en_US&id=com.playrix.homescapes
- Project Makeover shows how a stronger identity comes from a fantasy beyond the board itself. The board is the engine, but character glow-up, room style, and avatar expression are the fantasy.
  Source: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.bgg.jump
- Toon Blast demonstrates the value of unique level goals, obstacles, stars, teams, and rewards even without a renovation-heavy shell.
  Source: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?hl=en_IE&id=net.peakgames.toonblast
- Tropical match games such as Tropic Match, Seaside Resort, and Hawaii Match-3 Mania validate the island-renovation fantasy directly.
  Sources:
  https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?hl=en_NZ&id=com.rebelgames.tropicmatch
  https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?hl=en_US&id=com.global.xxsx.google
  https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?gl=US&hl=en_US&id=com.g5e.hawaiipg.android

## What to copy

- Copy the genre structure:
  - strong main menu
  - clear booster language
  - room or yard restoration after wins
  - obstacles that change board texture
  - juicy combo feedback
  - stars, coins, chests, and daily reward loops
- Do not copy:
  - branded characters
  - exact board items
  - exact booster names
  - exact art kits
  - exact music tracks

## Island-yard version

- Fantasy: bring an overgrown tropical family yard back to life one zone at a time.
- First restoration zones:
  - front gate and path
  - wash yard
  - fruit stand corner
  - cookout deck
  - domino table nook
  - hammock garden
- Match items should feel local and tactile:
  - hibiscus bloom
  - lime
  - sea glass
  - woven fan
  - mango slice

## Power-up set

- 4 in a row: Machete Rocket
- 5 in an L or T: Coconut Blast
- 2x2 square or targeted special: Firefly Propeller
- 5 in a row: Carnival Orb
- Consumables:
  - Yard Hammer
  - Free Swap
  - Extra Moves

## Obstacles

- Crates
- Vines
- Mud
- Coconuts
- Locked coolers
- Sand piles
- Lantern strings
- Water buckets that need repeated hits

## Asset and audio sourcing

- Kenney UI Pack is CC0 and is safe for placeholders, HUD parts, and buttons.
  Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/ui-pack
- Kenney UI Audio is CC0 and is good for click, confirm, and interface effects.
  Source: https://www.kenney.nl/assets/ui-audio
- Pixabay allows music, sound effects, and images in larger creative works, including apps and games, but not standalone redistribution. Keep the download link and license summary with each asset.
  Sources:
  https://pixabay.com/service/faq/
  https://pixabay.com/service/license-summary/

## Recommended build order

1. Menu and navigation shell
2. Real music and UI sound pack
3. Booster logic and obstacle framework
4. Restoration map with unlockable yard zones
5. Better tile art, VFX, and win celebration
